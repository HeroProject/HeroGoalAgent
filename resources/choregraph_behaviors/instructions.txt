1) Open behavior in Choregraphe 2.8.5.10.
2) Open the properties of the behavior and remove the number-letter combination behind the name in the Application-ID field (e.g. remove "-8e1ea8" in "elephant-8e1ea8" to make "elephant" for the elephant behavior).
3) "Package and install current package onto robot" by clicking the button (with that name) in the robot application tab (by default in the lower right of the screen).
